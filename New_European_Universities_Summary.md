## 🌍 Regional Insights

### Netherlands (TU Delft)
- **Pros:** Easy PR, English everywhere, bike culture, high quality of life
- **Cons:** High tuition for non-EU ($28k/year), moderate salaries

### Germany (RWTH Aachen)
- **Pros:** Almost free tuition, easy PR, strong industry, automotive AI
- **Cons:** German helpful for daily life, lower salaries than UK/Switzerland

### UK (UCL, Edinburgh)
- **Pros:** 1-year programs, English, strong AI scene, DeepMind connections
- **Cons:** High tuition, Brexit visa changes, medium PR difficulty

### Switzerland (EPFL)
- **Pros:** Highest salaries, low tuition, top research, beautiful
- **Cons:** Very hard PR (10+ years), expensive living, French helpful

### Nordics (KTH, DTU, Aalto)
- **Pros:** Best work-life balance, good salaries, easy-medium PR, English-friendly
- **Cons:** High tuition for non-EU (except Aalto), cold weather, smaller tech scenes
