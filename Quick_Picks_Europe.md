## 🎓 By Country Characteristics

### 🇩🇪 Germany (RWTH Aachen, TUM Munich)
**Best For:** Low cost + Easy PR + Strong industry
- Almost free tuition (€350-12k/year)
- Easy visa and PR (3-5 years)
- Strong automotive/engineering AI
- German helpful but not required
- Salaries: €66-72k

### 🇨🇭 Switzerland (ETH Zurich, EPFL)
**Best For:** Highest salaries + Top research
- Very low tuition (~$1.5k/year)
- Highest salaries in Europe ($138k)
- World-class research
- Hard PR (10+ years)
- Expensive living

### 🇬🇧 UK (Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, Edinburgh)
**Best For:** 1-year programs + English + AI ecosystem
- Fast ROI (1-year programs)
- Strong AI scene (DeepMind, etc.)
- High tuition (£42-45k)
- Medium PR difficulty (3-6 years)
- Salaries: £75-87k

### 🇳🇱 Netherlands (TU Delft)
**Best For:** Easy PR + English + Quality of life
- Easy PR (2-5 years)
- Everyone speaks English
- Strong robotics
- Moderate tuition (€26k/year)
- Salaries: €56k

### 🇸🇪 Sweden (KTH Stockholm)
**Best For:** Work-life balance + Startup scene
- Best work-life balance
- Strong startup ecosystem
- Medium PR (3-6 years)
- High tuition for non-EU (SEK 360k)
- Salaries: SEK 650k

### 🇩🇰 Denmark (DTU Copenhagen)
**Best For:** High salaries + Quality of life
- High salaries (DKK 585k)
- Green tech focus
- Medium PR (4-7 years)
- Moderate tuition (DKK 112k)
- Expensive living

### 🇫🇮 Finland (Aalto)
**Best For:** Easiest PR + Affordable
- Easiest PR in Europe (2-4 years)
- Moderate tuition (€15k/year)
- Good quality of life
- Smaller tech scene
- Salaries: €60k

## 💸 Scholarship Strategy

### For LPDP (Must Return 2+ Years)
All universities eligible - choose based on career goals

### For No Return Requirement
1. **Erasmus Mundus** → TU Delft
2. **Swedish Institute** → KTH Stockholm
3. **DAAD** → RWTH Aachen or TUM
4. **EPFL Excellence** → EPFL
5. **ETH ESOP** → ETH Zurich

### For UK (Chevening requires return)
- **Chevening** → Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, Edinburgh
- **Spärck AI** → Oxford, Imperial, UCL, Edinburgh (NEW 2026)
- **Commonwealth** → All UK universities

## 🚨 Important Notes

### Tuition Differences
- **Germany:** Almost free (€350-12k)
- **Switzerland:** Very low (~$1.5k)
- **Finland:** Moderate (€15k)
- **Denmark:** Moderate (DKK 112k)
- **Sweden:** High for non-EU (SEK 360k)
- **Netherlands:** High for non-EU (€26k)
- **UK:** Very high (£42-45k)

### PR Difficulty
- **Easiest:** Finland (2-4 years), Netherlands (2-5 years), Germany (3-5 years)
- **Medium:** Sweden (3-6 years), UK (3-6 years), Denmark (4-7 years)
- **Hard:** Switzerland (10+ years)

### Language Requirements
- **English Only:** UK, Netherlands, Nordics
- **English + Local Helpful:** Germany, Switzerland
- **Local Important:** Switzerland (French/German)
