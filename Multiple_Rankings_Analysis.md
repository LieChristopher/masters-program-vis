## 🎯 WHY MULTIPLE RANKINGS MATTER

### The Problem with QS for Chinese Universities:
- **QS heavily weights reputation surveys** (50% of score)
- **Underranks Chinese universities** in CS/AI
- **Example:** Tsinghua is #20 in QS but **#1 in CS/AI research output**

### Solution: Use Multiple Rankings
1. **QS** - Overall reputation, good for Western universities
2. **THE** - Research-focused, better for Chinese universities
3. **CSRankings** - Pure CS/AI research output (publication-based)
4. **US News CS** - Computer Science specific rankings
