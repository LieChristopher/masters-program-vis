## Key Takeaways from Research

1. **Affordability doesn't mean lower quality**: ETH Zurich and Tsinghua offer world-class education at fraction of US/UK costs

2. **Duration varies significantly**: 1-year UK programs vs 2-year European programs vs flexible US options (12-24 months)

3. **Market demand is strong**: 53% increase in AI jobs, significant salary premiums for AI skills

4. **Location matters for career**: 
   - US programs → US tech industry
   - European programs → EU opportunities + 18-month job search visa (Germany)
   - Asian programs → Growing Asian tech markets

5. **Program focus varies**:
   - Research-heavy: MIT, Stanford, Tsinghua, Oxford, Cambridge
   - Industry-oriented: CMU, Imperial, NUS
   - Balanced: ETH Zurich, TUM

6. **Funding opportunities exist**: Scholarships, assistantships, and industry partnerships can significantly reduce costs

7. **Admission is competitive**: Top programs have acceptance rates from 5-27%, requiring excellent academic records

8. **Work authorization matters**: Consider post-graduation work rights when choosing location (US OPT, UK graduate visa, EU job-seeking visas)
