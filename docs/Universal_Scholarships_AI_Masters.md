## UK Government Scholarships (Open to Multiple Countries)

### 4. Commonwealth Scholarships ⭐ EXCELLENT OPTION

**Overview:**
UK government scholarship for citizens of Commonwealth countries (Indonesia is a Commonwealth member).

**Coverage:**
- Full tuition fees
- Monthly living allowance: £1,378 (£1,781 in London)
- Economy return airfare
- Warm clothing allowance
- Study travel grant
- Additional grants for essential expenditure

**Eligible Programs:**
- Master's degrees at UK universities
- Computer Science, AI, Data Science included
- Universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL, Edinburgh, etc.

**Eligibility:**
- Citizen or refugee of Commonwealth country (Indonesia qualifies)
- Bachelor's degree
- Strong academic record
- Development focus (must show how studies benefit home country)
- Unable to afford UK study without scholarship

**Themes (2026-2027):**
- Science and technology for development ← Relevant for AI
- Strengthening health systems
- Promoting global prosperity
- Strengthening global peace, security and governance
- Strengthening resilience and response to crises
- Access, inclusion and opportunity

**Application:**
- Through CSC Central system
- Requires nominator
- No application fee
- Deadline: Typically December for September/October start
- Studies begin: September/October 2026

**Types:**
1. **Commonwealth Master's Scholarships** - For low/middle-income countries
2. **Commonwealth Shared Scholarships** - Partnership with UK universities

## Important Notes

1. **Multiple Applications:** Apply to multiple scholarships simultaneously. Don't put all eggs in one basket.

2. **Admission First:** Many scholarships require admission to the university first. Start with admission applications.

3. **Deadlines Vary:** Track deadlines carefully. Some are months before program start.

4. **Partial Funding:** Some scholarships are partial. Consider combining multiple sources.

5. **Service Bonds:** Some scholarships (especially Singapore) require work commitments after graduation. Read terms carefully.

6. **English Proficiency:** Most require IELTS 6.5-7.5 or TOEFL 90-100+. Prepare early.

7. **GRE:** Some programs require GRE, others don't. Check specific requirements.

8. **Research Funding:** For research-focused Master's, research assistantships may be available. Contact potential supervisors.
