## 📊 RANKING SOURCES

### Primary Rankings

**1. QS Rankings (qs.com)**
- Most widely used globally
- Bias: Favors research output and international reputation
- Best for: Overall university reputation

**2. Times Higher Education (THE) (timeshighereducation.com)**
- Strong for research-focused programs
- Bias: Emphasizes research citations; favors English-speaking universities
- Best for: Research-oriented programs

**3. CSRankings (csrankings.org)**
- Computer Science research-focused
- Bias: Purely research publication-based
- Best for: Research output validation, AI-specific rankings

**4. US News CS Rankings**
- Computer Science specific rankings
- Best for: CS program quality

### Specialized Sources

**5. MastersPortal.com / FindAMasters.com**
- Program-specific comparisons
- Best for: Program details and student feedback

**6. Forbes (forbes.com)**
- Value-focused rankings
- Best for: ROI and affordability analysis

### Key Biases to Watch

| Bias Type | Impact | Mitigation |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Regional bias | UK/US-focused sites undervalue Asian universities | Cross-reference with CSRankings |
| Research vs. teaching | May miss universities with strong industry connections | Check employer feedback |
| Outdated data | Costs and program details change rapidly | Verify directly with universities |

---

## 🎯 WHY MULTIPLE RANKINGS MATTER

### The Problem with QS for Chinese Universities:
- QS heavily weights reputation surveys (50% of score)
- Underranks Chinese universities in CS/AI
- Example: Tsinghua is #20 in QS but **#1 in CS/AI research output**

### Solution: Use Multiple Rankings
1. **QS** - Overall reputation, good for Western universities
2. **THE** - Research-focused, better for Chinese universities
3. **CSRankings** - Pure CS/AI research output (publication-based)
4. **US News CS** - Computer Science specific rankings

## ✅ RECOMMENDED APPROACH

**Cross-reference at least 2-3 sources:**
1. QS Computer Science rankings (overall reputation)
2. CSRankings (research validation)
3. Direct university websites (current costs, program details)

**For AI specifically:**
- Use QS Computer Science rankings
- Check CSRankings for research output
- Verify program details on university websites
- Check employer feedback and graduate outcomes

---

**Key Takeaway:** Chinese universities are world leaders in AI research but underranked in reputation-based rankings like QS. Use CSRankings or US News CS for accurate AI program assessment!
