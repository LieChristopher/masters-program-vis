## 🇦🇺 AUSTRALIA (4 Universities)

### Why Australia for Indonesian Students?
- **English-speaking** - No language barrier
- **Easy PR path** - 2-4 years via skilled migration (189/190 visa)
- **Close to Indonesia** - 6-7 hour flight, similar timezone
- **High salaries** - AUD 130-140k ($87-94k USD)
- **Quality of life** - Safe, multicultural, good weather
- **Strong AI research** - Especially Melbourne and ANU

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES

### Australia PR Path:
1. Graduate with Master's degree
2. Get post-study work visa (2-4 years)
3. Work in skilled occupation
4. Apply for skilled migration (189/190 visa)
5. **Timeline: 2-4 years total**

### Canada PR Path:
1. Graduate with Master's degree
2. Get PGWP (Post-Graduation Work Permit) - 3 years
3. Work for 1 year in skilled occupation
4. Apply for Express Entry (CEC - Canadian Experience Class)
5. **Timeline: 1-3 years total (FASTEST in the world)**

### Key Difference:
- **Canada:** Can apply for PR after just 1 year of work
- **Australia:** Need 2-4 years depending on points

---

**Recommendation:** If PR is your top priority, **Canada is the clear winner**. If you want to stay close to Indonesia, **Australia is better**.
